What is Online Internship?

Current times necessitate the use of virtual offices and virtual jobs, particularly with the advent of Covid-19 and the subsequent various safety restrictions.

Online Internship also known as Remote Internship or Virtual Internship is a work experience program where the participant gains experience while working in a remote setting through a computer or telephone, and is not physically present at the job location. It is a work experience that takes place online instead of commuting into an office each day. This means working, handling projects, taking phone calls and having meetings, all from anywhere you are once you have an internet connection and can communicate with your employer through a range of online resources and key communication platforms.
