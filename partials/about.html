About Us
Director’s Letter

Global Interns is a student-mediator agency, connecting students to internship jobs and career opportunities. We started as student recruiters, assisting students to get placements in schools abroad. This awakened in us a strong rapport and affinity with young people. We came to understand and empathize with their particular aspirations; especially those who had a great zeal to soar above the mediocre.

At Global Interns, we are Rethinking Internships and decided to go a step further and provide aid for youths beyond schooling. We additionally help prepare and place the young people for work, during school years and even after graduation, through Internship.

In the past few years, we have pooled eclectic resources together and grown our portfolio, both on site and online. We have organized workshops, camps, conferences, and various programs for Youth. Our Programs are mainly focused on developing Youth’s talents, mentoring and coaching, and creating opportunities for learning and sharing experiences. Our participants emerge from these Programs better envisioned, empowered and energized.

We dare say that Global Interns graduates are of great use to the organizations in which they intern. However, all these are incomplete without the participation of parents, corporations, global companies, business owners, and government agencies who provide the internship opportunities and are an integral part of the Global Interns Hub. We count on and appreciate your support. Together we are a pillar that props up the young ones, aiding them to arise and stand.

Welcome to Global Interns.

Yours,

Olufunmilayo Modupe Founder, Global Interns

Meet the Founder
Olufunmilayo Modupe

Funmi is a vibrant and engaging personality. A core professional and education enthusiast, she can also be described as humanitarian and empathetic in her orientation. Having acquired varying degrees in Geography & Regional Planning and Global Marketing, both locally and internationally, she has amassed over twenty-five years of experience, serving in various capacities in the Banking, Public Relations and Publishing industries.

In early 2013, Funmi founded a subsidiary firm Schoolingyonda Services, an organization birthed from passion and the need to fill identified gaps she picked up while placing her teenage children in schools abroad. This experience also exposed her to the misinformation and diverse challenges that families face while trying to place their children in schools generally and abroad in particular.

Funmi believes strongly that Education is an on-going endeavor and not limited to age or the classroom, which is the very reason Global Interns Company was launched. In addition to creating opportunities for students to gain working experience, it has become paramount to deploy resources and motivate young people towards work readiness.
