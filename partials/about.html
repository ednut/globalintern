Who is Global Interns
A Director’s letter

Global Interns is a student-mediator agency, connecting students to internship jobs and career opportunities.

We started out as student recruiters, assisting students to get placements in schools abroad.

This awakened in us a strong rapport and affinity with young people. We came to not just understand, but to also emphasize with, their particular aspirations; especially those who had great zeal to soar above the mediocre.

At Global Interns, we decided to go a step further and provide aid and support for these Youth beyond schooling. We additionally help prepare and place the young people for work, during school years and even after graduation, through Internship.

We have in the past few years pooled eclectic resources together and grown our portfolio, both on site and online. We have organized workshops, camps, conferences, and various programs for Youth. Our Programs are mainly focused on developing Youth’s talents, mentoring and coaching, and creating opportunities for learning and sharing experiences. Our participants emerge from these Programs better envisioned, empowered and energized. We dare say that Global Interns graduates are of great use to the Organizations in which they intern.

These are the reasons “why we do the things we do”

We are here to intentionally improve work readiness in young people who acknowledge they need help and are willing to pursue success.

All this is however incomplete without the participation of parents, corporations, business owners, establishments and Government agencies who provide the internship opportunities, and are an integral part of the Global Interns Hub. We count on and appreciate your support. Together we are a pillar that props up the young ones, aiding them to arise and stand.

Welcome to Global Interns.

Yours faithfully,

Olufunmilayo Modupe
Director
Our Mission
To provide a hub that gives key orientation to young people in Africa, and also aid them with excellent opportunities, to participate effectively in the workplace through internship.
Our vision
Seeing increasing numbers of students and graduates acquire business knowledge and social skills under the tutelage of industry experts, which will ultimately help in their subsequent employability and career development.
