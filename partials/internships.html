What is Remote Internship?

Current times necessitate the use of virtual offices and virtual jobs, particularly with the advent of Covid-19 and the subsequent various safety restrictions.

Online Internship also known as Remote Internship or Virtual Internship is a work experience program where the participant gains experience while working in a remote setting through a computer or telephone, and is not physically present at the job location. It is a work experience that takes place online instead of commuting into an office each day. This means working, handling projects, taking phone calls and having meetings, all from anywhere you are once you have an internet connection and can communicate with your employer through a range of online resources and key communication platforms.

Global Interns prepares students and graduates for the new digital workspace through virtual internships, giving a first-hand experience of remote working with, and in, reputable businesses. We help position you for your dream job and career potentials.

Why consider this?
  1. Accessibility and Flexibility: You can work from wherever you are, at your own time, once you have a computer or phone with an internet connection. Online internship work eliminates the task of getting to a physical office and back.
  2. Competitive edge: Competition for entry-level job openings is very high. To be noticed, your Resume needs to stand out and it surely will if it reads that you have work experience through online internship.
  3. Finances: An online internship gives you the opportunity to work and also save money used on transportation. Moreso, some companies even pay interns a token amount.
