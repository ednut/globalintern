Internships are usually arranged by third-party organizations that recruit interns on behalf of the Companies. This is a key role of Global Interns.

What is Remote Internship?

Remote Internship is also known as Online Internship or Virtual Internship is a work experience program where the participant gains experience while working in a remote setting through a computer or telephone, and is not physically present at the job location.

This means working, handling projects, taking phone calls and having meetings, all from anywhere you are once you have an internet connection and can communicate with your employer through a range of online resources and key communication platforms.

Global Interns prepares students and graduates for the new digital workspace through remote internships, giving a first-hand experience of remote working with, and in, reputable businesses. We help position you for your dream job and career potentials.

Why Remote?
  1. Accessibility and Flexibility: You can work from wherever you are, at your own time, once you have a computer or phone with an internet connection.
  2. Competitive edge: Competition for entry-level job openings is very high. To be noticed, your Resume needs to stand out and it surely will if it reads that you have remote work experience.
  3. Finances: It allows you to work and also save money that would have been used on transportation. More so, some companies even pay interns a stipend.
