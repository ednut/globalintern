Global Interns serves as a hub for training, preparing and placing eligible young people in an internship programs.

Online internship in general is a very cost-effective source of obtaining interested and motivated labor. You will not need to provide a workspace, office tools and equipment and other employer obligations, rather you will obtain candidates who can get the small tasks completed at little cost to you.

Employing online interns is also a tremendous social service and responsibility to take up as you will be providing the upcoming ones with a great foundation and experience for future employment. If you wish to employ grounded interns at very little cost to your company, we are your best plug.

We are equally open to discussions of an alternative In Person Internship if that will be your preference. We are always on the lookout for and welcome interested host-companies for our interns.
